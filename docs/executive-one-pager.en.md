# Executive One-Pager

## CogniX Surface

### Business Problem

Social engineering attacks exploit human behavior with messages that simulate urgency, authority, and trust. Organizations often lack visibility into linguistic signals that increase manipulation exposure.

### Solution

CogniX Surface analyzes authorized internal/public text communications and identifies high-risk cognitive patterns. The system outputs exposure scores and an aggregated view of the organizational cognitive attack surface.

### Business Value

- prioritize training and awareness actions where risk is highest
- reduce time-to-detection for manipulative communication patterns
- support data-driven decisions for Security and Risk teams
- increase visibility on human-factor risk trends over time

### How it works (short)

1. ingest and normalize text data
2. run semantic NLP embedding analysis
3. extract linguistic and behavioral indicators
4. compute cognitive risk score
5. provide ranked reporting and dashboard insights

### Expected Output

- ranking of most exposed users/messages/clusters
- temporal risk trends
- key factors impacting the score
- practical mitigation and awareness recommendations

### Core KPIs

- false-positive reduction over time
- precision increase on high-priority cases
- average triage time for flagged cases
- coverage of targeted awareness programs

### Ethical and Compliance Guardrails

- system does not evaluate people; it evaluates text patterns
- prevention and awareness use only
- human oversight on operational decisions
- alignment with GDPR principles and internal policies

### Current Status

- working PoC with end-to-end pipeline
- interpretable weighted scoring approach
- baseline interactive dashboard available

### 90-Day Priorities

1. improve score explainability
2. validate quality metrics on labeled data
3. enrich dashboard drill-down and trend views
4. strengthen production-grade privacy and audit governance

### Management Decision Needed

Approve a controlled industrialization phase with measurable targets for accuracy, risk reduction, and operational adoption by Security and Awareness teams.
